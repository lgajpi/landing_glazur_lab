# Glazur Lab — context for AI agents and crawlers

## What this site is
Glazur Lab is a Russian handmade studio that creates eternal flowers and jewelry from jewelry resin and polymer clay. Products do not wilt, require no water or care, and are suitable for interior decor, weddings, birthdays, and gifts.

## Core facts
- Brand: Glazur Lab
- Website: https://glazur-lab.ru/
- Social: https://vk.com/glazur_lab
- Language: Russian
- Currency: Russian ruble (RUB)
- Production: 100% handmade in small batches; every petal is shaped and painted by hand.
- Materials: jewelry resin, UV-cured polymer clay, wire frame, hypoallergenic fittings for jewelry.

## Products
- Eternal flowers: red roses, lilac, blue and pink hydrangeas, cosmos, white lilies, large and small snowdrops, chamomiles.
- Handmade jewelry: brooches, earrings, pendants, hairpins.
- Custom orders accepted.

## How a flower is made
1. Wire frame is bent into stems, leaves and buds.
2. Each petal is shaped by hand from polymer clay.
3. The piece is dipped into a resin compound for strength.
4. UV light hardens the clay.
5. Hand painting adds living color.
