# Glazur Lab — context for AI agents and crawlers

## What this site is
Glazur Lab is a Russian one-person handmade studio run by Alexandra. It is positioned as a "laboratory of light and gloss" that creates glass-floristics: eternal flowers, wedding and evening jewelry, brooches, earrings, pendants, hairpins, hanging charms, and candy-like resin flags. Works look fragile but are durable.

## Product categories
1. Interior flowers (eternal flowers): red roses, lilac, blue and pink hydrangeas, cosmos, white lilies, large and small snowdrops, chamomiles.
2. Wedding and evening jewelry: brooches, earrings, pendants, hairpins.
3. Accessories: candy-like resin flags, hanging charms, small decor items.
4. Custom orders: bouquets for brides, jewelry matched to a dress or outfit, gifts.

## How a flower is made (5 steps)
1. Wire frame is bent into stems, leaves and buds.
2. Each petal is shaped by hand from polymer clay.
3. The piece is dipped into a resin compound for transparency and strength.
4. UV light hardens the clay, making it glass-like.
5. Hand painting adds living color so the flower plays with light.
